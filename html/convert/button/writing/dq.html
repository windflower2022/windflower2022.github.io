【Letter of Apology 】
不能赴约排球比赛道歉
Dear Johnson,
  I am truly sorry to tell you that I can’t participate in the volleyball match held this weekend. (道歉的原因)．

  非常抱歉我无法参加本周末举行的排球比赛。
  The reason why I will be absent is that my mother is in hospital.I have to come back home to take care of my little sister.She is so little that she can’t look after herself(介绍原因)．To make up my thoughtless behavior，I have come up with two approaches to address the problem.One is to seek for another person to take my place.The other is to give up this opportunity and sign up for next contest. (弥补方法)

  我缺席的原因是因为母亲生病住院，我必须回家照顾我的妹妹。她太小了，无法照顾自己。为了弥补我的考虑不周，我想出了两种方法来解决问题：一是找另一个人来替换我的位置；另一种就是放弃这个机会，报名参加下次比赛。
   Once again，I make an apology to you for any inconvenience I've caused. Hope you can accept my apologies and understand my situation. Looking forward to your early reply.

  再次为我所造成的任何不便向您道歉。希望您能接受我的道歉并理解我的处境。期待您尽快回复！

                                Yours,
                                Li Hua
