   The Dragon Boat Festival falls on the 5th day of the 5th lunar month . It is aimed to remember Qu Yuan,a famous poet and raise the awareness of respecting traditional festivals .During the festival , We can join in the Dagon Boat race,eat varieties of Zongzi ,which are pretty delicious .Meanwhile, we can learn some knowledge about the traditional culture of Dragon Boat Festival .
