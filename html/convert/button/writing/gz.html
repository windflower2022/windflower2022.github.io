【Letter of Inform】
【回复告知世界读书日调查活动情况】
Dear Sam，
  I'm more than delighted to know that you're interested in the result of reading research launched by my class,so I'm writing to share more relevant information with you.

  得知你们对我们班发起的阅读研究的结果感兴趣，我非常高兴。所以写信与你分享更多相关的信息。
  In order to have a better understanding of students reading life and appeal to them to pay more attention to reading,our class organized this activity on April 23rd the World Reading Day.Wewere divided into 8 groups at first. Then I,together with another 3 classmates,was going to the playground to do the research.Finally we returned to our classroom to make a summary . It's not easy to finish this task.You must be outgoing and brave to invite students to do the questionnaire and when they reply,you're supposed to be patient and wear a smile.

  为了更好地了解学生的阅读生活，呼吁他们更多地关注阅读，我们班在4月23日世界阅读日组织了这次活动。首先我们被分成8个小组。然后我和另外3个同学一起去操场做研究。最后我们回到教室做了一个总结。完成这项任务不容易。你必须外向并勇敢的邀请学生做问卷调查，当他们回答，你应该是耐心和面带微笑。
  Hope my information can be useful for you and you can consult me for any other doubt if you have.
  Best wishes!

  希望我的信息对你有用，如果你有任何其他疑问，可以咨询我。致以最好的祝福！

                                Yours,
                                Li Hua

  【写信告知茶文化研学活动信息】
Dear Lucy,
  Knowing that you will join us in the coming research activity on tea culture, I am writing to offer you some important information.

  知道您将加入我们即将举行的茶文化研究活动，我写信给您提供一些重要的信息。
  The activity will take place in Duichuan Tea Garden this Friday. We are going to meet at the school gate at 8:00 on Friday morning and go there by bus. There many interesting programs are waiting for us. At first , we will learn the skills of tea picking and tea making, which may be a little challenging. Then will come to the most exciting part — enjoying the tea. And to our joy, photo taking is allowed during the whole process! By the way, remember to take some food, drinks, as well as your hat with you.

  活动将于本周五在对川茶园举行。我们打算星期五早上8点在学校门口见面，然后坐公共汽车去那里。有很多有趣的节目在等着我们。首先，我们将学习采茶和泡茶的技巧，这可能有点难度。然后就到了最精彩的部分，品茶。让我们高兴的是，在整个过程中都允许拍照！顺便说一下，记得带一些食物，饮料，还有你的帽子。
  It is my sincere hope that you will have a great time.

  衷心希望你们玩得愉快。

                                Yours,
                                Li Hua

  【介绍孔子】
Dear Jim,
  Your letter came to me this morning. I am more than delighted that you have developed an interest in Chinese history.

  你的信今天早上到了，很高兴你对中国历史产生了兴趣。
  When it comes to my favourite figure in Chinese history, it must be Confucius,Chinas most famous teacher,philosopher, and political theorist.He has made the greatest contribution to Chinese culture of anyone in history. Many of his words of wisdom that have been passed down from generation to generation have made a profound impact on the Chinese.In fact,he inspires me to become a teacher in the future for the purpose of cultivating talents.

  说到中国历史上我最喜欢的人物，一定是孔子，中国最著名的老师、哲学家和政治理论家。他对中国文化做出了历史上最伟大的贡献。他的许多智慧之言代代相传，对中国人产生了深远的影响，事实上，他启发我将来要成为一名教师，培养人才。
  If you want to know more about Confucius, please let me know. I can find you some books for you !
  Best wishes!

  如果你想了解更多关于孔子的知识，请告诉我。我可以给你找些书。致以最好的祝福！

                                Yours,
                                Li Hua
