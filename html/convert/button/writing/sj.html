【Letter of Application】
成都第32届大运会志愿者申请
Dear sir,
   I am Li Hua,a student from Xin Hua middle school.Having known that the 32nd summer universide will be held in Chengdu on July 20th.I am more than delighted to apply to be a volunteer . Here are my advantages.

  我叫李华，是新华中学的学生。得知第32届夏季大学生运动会将于7月20日在成都举行。我非常高兴申请成为一名志愿者。 我的优势如下：
  First of all,I have a good command of English which makes me communicate with foreigners fluently.What’s more,I am outgoing and friendly,so I can easily get on well with others.When they are in trouble.I am willing to help them.Last but not least,I am keen on sports and learn about varieties of rules.

  首先，我有良好的英语水平，这使我能够流利地与外国人交流。更重要的是，我外向而友好，所以我可以很容易地与他人相处。当他们有困难的时候，我愿意帮助他们。最后但并非最不重要的，我热衷于运动，并学习各种规则。
  All in all,I am quarlified for the position.I would appreciate it if you could take my application into consideration.Looking forward to your early reply.

  总之，我有资格担任这个职位，如果你能考虑我的申请，我将不胜感激。期待你的早日答复。

                                Yours,
                                Li Hua
