May 21st this year marks the first International Tea Day, which was named officially by the United Nations on November 27th, 2019. To celebrate the festival, a number of events took place at the Chinese Businessman Museum in Beijing on Thursday.
  今年的5月21日是首个国际茶日，这个节日于2019年11月27日由联合国正式命名。为了庆祝这个节日，周四在北京的中国商人博物馆举办了一系列活动。

  The chairman of the China Culture Promotion Society addressed the opening ceremony. "As a main promoter of the International Tea Day, the birthplace of tea and the largest tea-producing country, China has a responsiblility to work with other countries to promote the healthy development of the tea industry. It can help to build a community with a shared future for mankind," he said.
  中国文化促进会主席在开幕式上发表讲话。他说：“作为国际茶日的主要推动者、茶叶的发源地和最大的产茶国家，中国有责任与其他国家共同促进茶叶产业的健康发展。这可以帮助构建人类命运共同体。”

   The "First International Tea Day Tea Road Cooperative Initiative" issued（发布）at the ceremony calls for people working in the tea industry to come together to promote international cooperation and cultural exchanges. A four-year tea promotion—Tea Road Cooperative Plan—was also issued in accordance with the initiative.
  在仪式上发布的“第一个国际茶日茶道合作倡议”呼吁从事茶叶行业的人们团结起来，促进国际合作和文化交流。同时还制定了为期四年的茶业推广计划——茶道合作计划。

   To strengthen the connection with young people, the event included a number of public promotional activities on social media, inviting twenty-nine tea professionals from around the world to have thirty-six hours of uninterrupted live broadcasts.
  为了加强与年轻人的联系，本次活动包括了许多社交媒体上的宣传活动，邀请来自全球的29名茶叶专业人士进行36小时不间断的直播。

   The Chinese Ancient Tea Museum was officially unveiled（揭幕）at the ceremony, opening its first exhibition: The Avenue of Truth—A Special Exhibition of Pu'er Tea.
  中国古茶博物馆在仪式上正式揭幕，并开展了其第一场展览：“真理大道——普洱茶特别展览”。
