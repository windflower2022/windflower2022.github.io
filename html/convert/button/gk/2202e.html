We all know that cycling is a great exercise. A doctor tells me people who live the longest are dancers and cyclists. Maybe it is because of the combination of fresh air, smooth movement and exercise. When you ride a bicycle, you don't use petrol. So you are not producing carbon dioxide and not causing air pollution. Just see how cars have been taken over our cities.They often run at high speeds, which may put our lives in danger. And there are traffic jams, too. Our cities will be better places if we replace cars with bicycles.
我们都知道骑自行车是很好的锻炼方式。一位医生告诉我，最长寿的人是舞者和骑自行车的人。也许这是因为新鲜的空气、平滑的运动和锻炼的结合吧。当你骑自行车时，你不使用汽油，所以你不会产生二氧化碳，也不会导致空气污染。看看汽车是如何霸占我们的城市。它们经常高速行驶，可能会将我们的生命置于危险之中。而且还有交通拥堵的问题。如果我们用自行车代替汽车，我们的城市将会成为更美好的地方.
