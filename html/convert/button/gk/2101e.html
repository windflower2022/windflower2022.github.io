Last week our teacher asked us to fill in a questionnaire. One of the questions is：Who will you go to in times of trouble? Here are the results. Many students say they will talk to their friends or classmates because they're of the same age and can understand each other. Some will turn to their parents or teachers for help. Only a few choose to deal with the problems on their own. Their answers also show that they dislike talking to others. They keep very much to themselves. In my opinion, when in trouble, we should seek help from those we trust most.
上周我们的老师让我们填写了一份问卷，其中一个问题是：在遇到麻烦时你会向谁求助？以下是结果。很多学生表示他们会跟他们的朋友或同学倾诉，因为他们年龄相仿，可以互相理解。有些人会向他们的父母或老师寻求帮助。只有很少数人选择独自解决问题。他们的答案也表明他们不喜欢与他人交流，他们更喜欢保持沉默。我认为，在遇到困境时，我们应该向我们最信任的人寻求帮助。
