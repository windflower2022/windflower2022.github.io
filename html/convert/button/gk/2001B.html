Returning to a book you have read many times can feel like drinks with an old friend. There is a welcome familiarity — but also sometimes a slight suspicion that time has changed you both, and thus the relationship. But books do not change, people do. And that is what makes the act of rereading so rich and transformative.
  重新阅读一本你已经读过很多次的书，就像和一个老朋友喝酒一样。有一种令人欣慰的熟悉感，但有时也会稍微怀疑时间已经改变了你们两个人，从而改变了你们的关系。但是书籍不会改变，人会改变。这就是重新阅读如此丰富和变革性的原因。
  The beauty of rereading lies in the idea that our bond with the work is based on our present mental register. it is true, the older I get, the more I feel time has wings. But with reading, it is all about the present. it is about the now and what one contributes to the now, because reading is a give and take between author and reader. Each has to pull their own weight.
  重新阅读的美在于我们与作品的联系是基于我们当前的心理状态。没错，随着年龄的增长，我越来越感觉时间飞快。但是阅读是关于现在的，关于现在和每个人对现在的贡献，因为阅读是作者和读者之间的互动。每个人都必须承担自己的责任。
  There are three books I reread annually. The first, which I take to reading every spring is Emest Hemningway’s A Moveable Feast. Published in 1964, it is his classic memoir of 1920s Paris. The language is almost intoxicating (令人陶醉的), an aging writer looking back on an ambitious yet simpler time. Another is Annie Dillard’s Holy the Firm, her poetic 1975 ramble (随笔) about everything and nothing. The third book is Julio Cortazar’s Save Twilight: Selected Poems, because poetry. And because Cortazar.
  我每年都会重新阅读三本书。第一本是欧内斯特·海明威的《流动的盛宴》。这本1964年出版的经典回忆录讲述了20世纪20年代的巴黎。语言几乎令人陶醉，一个年迈的作家回顾了一个雄心勃勃但更简单的时代。另一本是安妮·戴拉德的《神圣的坚实》，她的1975年诗意漫游，关于一切和无关紧要的事情。第三本书是胡里奥·科尔塔萨尔的《拯救黄昏：选定诗歌》，因为诗歌。还因为科尔塔萨尔。
  While I tend to buy a lot of books, these three were given to me as gifts, which might add to the meaning I attach to them. But I imagine that, while money is indeed wonderful and necessary, rereading an author’s work is the highest currency a reader can pay them. The best books are the ones that open further as time passes. But remember, it is you that has to grow and read and reread in order to better understand your friends.
  虽然我倾向于购买很多书，但这三本书是别人送给我的礼物，这可能增加了我对它们的意义的附加值。但是我想象，虽然钱确实很好，也很必要，但是重新阅读作者的作品是读者可以付给他们的最高回馈。最好的书是随着时间的推移而进一步打开的书。但请记住，你必须成长、阅读和重新阅读，才能更好地理解你的朋友们。
