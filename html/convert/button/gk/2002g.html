Decorating with Plants, Fruits and Flowers for Chinese New Year
为中国新年装饰植物、水果和花卉
  Chinese New Year is a celebration marking the end of the winter season and the beginning of spring. This is why decorating with plants, fruits and flowers carries special significance. They represent the earth coming back to life and best wishes for new beginnings.
  中国新年是一个庆祝冬天结束和春天开始的庆祝活动。这就是为什么用植物、水果和花卉装饰具有特殊的意义。它们代表着地球的复苏和对新的开始的最美好的祝愿。
  These are some of the most popular in many parts of the country: Oranges: Orange trees are more than decoration; they are a symbol of good fortune and wealth. They make great gifs and you see them many times decorated with red envelopes and messages of good fortune.
  在这个国家的许多地方，这些都是最受欢迎的：橘子： 橘子树不仅仅是装饰，它们是好运和财富的象征。他们是很好的礼物，你会经常看到他们被红包和好运的信息装饰着。
  Bamboo: Chinese love their “Lucky Bamboo” plants and you will see them often in their homes and office.Certainly during the holiday period, this plant is a must. Bamboo plants are associated with health, abundance and a happy home. They are easy to care for and make great presents.
  竹子：中国人喜欢他们的"幸运竹"植物，你会经常在家里和办公室看到它们。当然在假日期间，这种植物是必须的。竹子与健康、富足和幸福的家息息相关。它们很容易照顾，而且是很好的礼物。
  Branches of Plum Blossoms : The beautiful long branches covered with pink-colored buds make fantastic decorations. The plum trees are the first to flower even as the snow is melting. They represent the promise of spring and a renewal of life.
  梅花枝：美丽的长枝上长满了粉红色的花莺，装饰得很漂亮。即使雪正在融化，梅树也会最先开花。它们代表着春的承诺和万物复苏。
